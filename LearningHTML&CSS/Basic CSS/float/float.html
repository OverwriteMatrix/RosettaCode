BOX 1

The morning sun filtered through the dense canopy of trees, casting dappled shadows across the forest floor. A light breeze rustled the leaves, carrying with it the scent of fresh pine and damp earth. Birds flitted from branch to branch, their melodies intertwining with the distant hum of a nearby stream. As the day unfolded, the world seemed to slow down, each moment stretching longer than the last.
