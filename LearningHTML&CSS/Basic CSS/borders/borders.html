Borders

The morning sun filtered through the dense canopy of trees, casting dappled shadows across the forest floor. A light breeze rustled the leaves, carrying with it the scent of fresh pine and damp earth. Birds flitted from branch to branch, their melodies intertwining with the distant hum of a nearby stream. As the day unfolded, the world seemed to slow down, each moment stretching longer than the last. Beneath the towering trees, hidden paths beckoned with the promise of new discoveries, and every step was an invitation to explore the mysteries of the wild. The air was thick with the rhythm of nature, timeless and ever-present, inviting all who wandered to lose themselves in its quiet embrace.
